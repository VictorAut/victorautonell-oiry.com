My name is Victor and I work with ceramics and wood to make unique pottery vessels and crafted branch flutes. My ceramics tend toward the ornate being hand-built, both detailed and methodical but with dissymmetry breaking with the notion of perfection in their design. My flutes are entire tree branches from various hardwoods, hollowed out, carved and tuned in the Native American style fLute.

I'm not trained in any arts, having taken the rather roundabout route of studying astrophysics. What was originally an approach to my artistic practice with a sense of helplessness (how do I do this?), has matured to a sense of being unconstrained in my approach to scuplting unique objects. Perhaps it is the physicist in me that seeks to observe the chaos of nature around us and accept the lent imprint of that in my own creation.

I've worked in the field of education for the past 3 years. My experience has been informed on the basis of understanding the nature of physical reality and communicating with others to transmit knowledge. I'm also an experienced data scientist with varied skills in the digital sphere. Like the website? — I coded it up from scratch myself!
